A montange of interviews with a range of Dallas musicians intercut with film of their rehersals and scenes of the Dallas landscape. The result is a positive view of the characters and the music which drives them.
At the premier in the Texas Theatre, Dallas on September 29 2018 the director explained that the first showing had been delayed after several mishaps occured at scheduled festivals. The version shown that night had been re-editted from the original which may explain the separate listing of a 2016 version.
