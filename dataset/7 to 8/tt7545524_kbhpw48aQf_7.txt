This movie chronicles 24 hours in the life of a family after the oldest son comes home from rehab one Christmas eve.The first part of the movie is good in it's illustration of the chaos and disruption drug addiction has on a family when one of their own is addicted.The story starts out strong but delves in to unbelievable scenarios such as when the addict son goes after the drug dealer after a break in and theft of the family pet.Good acting by both Julie Roberts and Lucas Hedges.
