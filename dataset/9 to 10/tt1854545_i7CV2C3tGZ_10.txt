This movie has it all, the ups and downs. At one moment it seems as if everything is going right and success is inevitable until another set back. His inner perseverance and determination along with friends and family has him chasing a dream which he will not be denied. This is something we all have in us and Chris brings it out. Chris Vaughn is a risk taker and who is always upbeat and funny through a ton of negative situations. This is a microcosm of life in which nothing is guaranteed but through hard work, support and an inner belief the journey can be accomplished. This movie will have you on your seat the entire time it has comedy, obstacles and roller coaster ride from start to finish.
