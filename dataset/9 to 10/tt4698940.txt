The Moleskin Diary is a shining example of what an indie film should be! A multiple Remi Awarding winning film at the Worldfest International Film Festival that is as interesting as it is compelling.Hannah Stock has instinctive talent and shines in her role as Maddy Jacobs-Stone. She has a real future in films, so keep an eye out for her.Zach Brown, Director and Producer does fabulous work. As a first time Director, his talent is impressive!
