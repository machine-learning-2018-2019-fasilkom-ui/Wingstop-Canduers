Small budget with a major punch! One pair of roommates live in a house whose previous occupant disappeared without a trace. Meanwhile, another pair of roommates find their apartment has become haunted-no one else is in the apartment, yet someone or something is there.This tense, intelligent sci fi thriller keeps you on the edge of your seat without resorting to violence or gore. Multiple plot threads merge into an innovative puzzle. You'll feel the characters' fear as they struggle to figure out what the heck is going on. This is a great first feature by an innovative new pair of writer/directors.
